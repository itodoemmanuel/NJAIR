Author Guidelines

Prepare and submit your manuscripts with clarity and confidence

Scope of the Journal

We accept original research in public administration, policy analysis, governance, and interdisciplinary topics aligned with administrative innovations.

Manuscript Formatting

  • Word format (.doc or .docx)
  • 12pt Times New Roman, 1.5 line spacing
  • APA referencing style
  • Include an abstract (150–250 words) and 3–5 keywords

Ethical Guidelines

Submissions must be original, cited properly, and not under review elsewhere. Authors must disclose any conflicts of interest and ensure ethical compliance.

Peer Review Process

All submissions undergo double-blind review by two reviewers. Acceptance is based on originality, clarity, relevance, and contribution.

Submission Process

Email your manuscript to njairjournal@gmail.com with subject: “NJAIR Submission.” Attach the manuscript and a brief cover letter.

Publication Fees

Accepted manuscripts incur a processing fee of ₦15,000. NJAIR remains open-access with no reading or download charges.

Author Responsibilities

  • Maintain accuracy and originality
  • Uphold ethical research standards
  • Engage with peer review feedback constructively

Reviewer Guidelines

Reviewers must be fair, constructive, and timely in their reviews. Confidentiality must be maintained throughout the review process.

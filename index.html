About NJAIR

The Nigerian Journal of Administration and Innovative Research (NJAIR) is an academic journal published by the Department of Public Administration, Rev. Fr. Moses Orshio Adasu University, Makurdi. It serves as a peer-reviewed platform for theoretical and empirical discourse on administration, leadership, innovation, governance, and public policy from local and global perspectives.

NJAIR aims to foster a community of researchers committed to advancing public sector reforms and innovation in Nigeria and across Africa.
